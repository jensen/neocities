Fonts

The fonts that are most safe to use are:

  • Arial / Helvetica
  • Times New Roman / Times
  • Courier New / Courier

Other options that usually work cross-platform are:

  • Palatino
  • Garamond
  • Bookman
  • Avant Garde

Fonts that work on Windows and MacOS but not Unix+X are:

  • Verdana
  • Georgia
  • Comic Sans MS
  • Trebuchet MS
  • Arial Black
  • Impact
